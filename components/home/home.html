Welcome to Atlas Cooperative

Hello, and welcome to Atlas Cooperative.

This site contains tools to help us draw and share maps of the worlds of our imaginations.

To draw a map, navigate to the Editor, click File | New ..., and, to start, select one of the built-in templates (Overland, Locale, or Site).

These templates contain built-in tools and styles that make it easier to get started.

I haven't written any tutorials (yet). I hope the tools aren't too confusing to use. If they are, my apologies.


The Catalog and Account sections haven't been developed yet.

This is where I intend, eventually, to put tools for sharing maps with friends and fellow cartographers (the cooperative part of Atlas Cooperative).

For now, you can save your maps to file and email them.


The Help section contains controls for setting preferences and an error log in case (😮) you encounter any bugs.

In the future, I may add an FAQ section and tutorials.


Everything here is free to use and my intent is that it remain so.

(Down the road, if these tools see a lot of use from a lot of people, I may introduce some kind of membership fee to offset hosting costs. Perhaps a guild with annual dues? TBD.)


I hope you enjoy this site and thanks for visiting!
